Schedule A · Privileges of the Member

What renewal affords

Article I

Perpetual verification

Cuteness formally certified by the Council and re-audited annually. The audit has one possible outcome.

Article II

Title retention

"The Superior Bwabwa" is held for life upon renewal. No challenger has ever been recognised. None will be.

Article III

Registry honours

The member's achievements are formally recorded, preserved in the Society archive, and read aloud at Council sessions.

Article IV

Council access

A direct line to both Elders, in perpetuity. Response times vary. Affection does not.

Annex D · Conditions of Renewal

The fine print

  1. Duration. Renewal extends membership until age eighteen, whereupon the member graduates to the Cute Adult division, subject to Council review.
  2. The Council. Comprises two Elders. Its decisions are final, unanimous, and binding on all parties, especially the member.
  3. Conduct. The member undertakes to remain the Superior Bwabwa at all times, school days included.
  4. Appeals. May be lodged in writing. They will be read with interest and declined with warmth.
  5. Cancellation. There is none. One is born into this.
